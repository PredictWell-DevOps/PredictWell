ABOUT PREDICTWELL

Early insight. Safer care.

PredictWell turns quick check-ins into clear, actionable risk signals for older adults and for athletes. We focus on **catching changes early** so families, coaches, and clinicians can act with confidence. Our two products — Eldercare Sentinel and Athletics Sentinel — blend wearable/behavioral data with weekly signals to surface what matters, when it matters.

How our services work

1) Simple inputs
  • Weekly 60–90s check-in (web or mobile)
  • Optional wearable data (SpO₂, HRV, sleep, steps)
  • Domain-specific questions (falls, dizziness; workload, pain)
2) Clear results
  • Low / Moderate / High quick risk view
  • “Why” explanation and contributing factors
  • Friendly patient summary + clinician detail
3) Actionable follow-ups
  • Personalized next steps (prehab, medication review, rest)
  • Alerts when trends worsen
  • Shareable PDF or dashboard view
