ABOUT

PredictWell Health.ai is a preventative health AI that transforms everyday signals—wearables, short weekly voice or gait check-ins, and simple phone-based inputs—into early alerts and personalized actions. By continuously learning your normal patterns in mobility, balance, sleep, and cognition, PredictWell detects small changes that may signal elevated risk. This allows for timely interventions to help prevent falls, hospitalizations, or sudden declines before they occur. The result is a calmer, smarter way to stay ahead of health risks—helping individuals maintain independence, families stay informed, and clinicians make proactive, data-driven decisions that improve wellbeing and quality of life.

Redefining Preventive Care with Adaptive AI

PredictWell Health.ai is redefining preventive care through adaptive artificial intelligence. Our mission is to close the gap between health data and real-world action—using AI to detect early risks, personalize interventions, and empower both families and professionals to make proactive, evidence-based decisions.

By combining multi-signal monitoring, explainable AI, and accessible design, PredictWell promotes health equity, longevity, and performance—from eldercare to elite sports—creating a smarter, more preventive future for every generation.
