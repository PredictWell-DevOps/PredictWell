How it works

Simple 1-2-3 flow modeled after best-in-class alert systems: press/record, we analyze, you get clear next steps. (Structure inspired by Medical Alert’s flow.)

1

Check-in or Press Help

Users complete a 60-second voice & gait check-in, or press a help button in the app if something feels off.

2

AI analyzes signals

Predictive models review trends from wearables + check-ins to detect fall, cognition, or overuse risk patterns.

3

Clear next steps

Get step-by-step guidance: call caregiver, do a balance drill, rest day for pitchers, or escalate if needed.
