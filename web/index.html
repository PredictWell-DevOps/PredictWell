About PredictWell

PredictWell Health.ai combines multi-signal wearables with brief, guided check-ins to surface risks earlier—before they become events. Our tools are tuned for two high-impact populations: older adults (falls, frailty, cognition, hospitalization) and athletes (workload balance, fatigue, return-to-play).

Designed with clinicians and coaches, our results emphasize clarity and next steps rather than raw scores. Data privacy and safety reviews are built-in.

Fast setup: connect wearables or use phone-only mode.
Low burden: 60–90s weekly check-ins with auto-reminders.
Actionable: clear flags, trend views, and suggested follow-ups.
